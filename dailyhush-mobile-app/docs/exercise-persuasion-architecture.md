# DailyHush Exercise Persuasion Architecture

## By Robert Cialdini

**Date:** November 4, 2025
**Purpose:** Maximize exercise completion rates through ethical application of the 7 principles of influence

---

## STRATEGIC OVERVIEW

Your users are in a state of **HIGH UNCERTAINTY** when they choose an exercise. This is your most persuadable moment. They're anxious, they don't know if this will work, and they're looking for ANY signal that they're making the right choice.

This is where the 7 principles become FORCE MULTIPLIERS.

**Critical Insight:** The moment someone selects an exercise is NOT when persuasion starts. It starts 30 seconds BEFORE they tap it. Pre-suasion is everything.
